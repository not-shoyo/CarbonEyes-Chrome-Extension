The internet has had a significant impact on carbon emissions, both positive and negative. On one hand, the internet has enabled people to communicate and work remotely, reducing the need for travel and therefore reducing greenhouse gas emissions. On the other hand, the energy consumption of the internet has grown dramatically in recent years, contributing to the overall increase in carbon emissions. As such with this tool we hope to help you utilize the internet in a more sustainable way, so that we can help mitigate its negative impact on the environment and help to create a more sustainable future.
